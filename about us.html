Welcome to Enviro-map

A researcher/data collector one must go to a specific place to collect information about the environmental factors. Factors that include: Temperature, Humidity, PM (Particulate Matter) 10, 2.5 and 1.0, Air Quality (if possible), VOC (Volatile Organic Carbon), Land Type and etc. Additionally, gathering data of some factors require very expensive gadgets/machineries, that not a lot of people have access to or can afford. All of these make the data collection process rather a very expensive, tedious, tiring and a time consuming process.

Our mission is to make a well organized, easy to access system that would make it a lot easier for everyone to find all their required information and stay updated with current events. People could also raise awareness about the environment based on the graphs provided by the system.
